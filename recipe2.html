Cheese-Coated Toast

Delicious, crispy, cheese-coated toast, perfect for breakfast, lunch, or a quick snack.

Ingredients:

For the Cheese Coating:

  • 45g Butter (for 3 slices of bread)
  • 36g Grated Cheese (for 3 slices of bread)

Additional Ingredients (Variations):

  • Sliced Bread
  • Sandwich Ham
  • Cheese slices
  • Egg
  • Nutella
  • Salt (optional)
  • Pepper (optional)

Instructions:

Cheese-Coated Toast Base:

  1. Mix the butter and grated cheese in a small bowl until well combined and smooth.
  2. Spread the cheese-butter mixture generously on one side of the sliced bread.
  3. Place the bread, cheese-coated side down, in a non-stick pan over low heat.
  4. Cook until the cheese coating is crispy and golden brown. This should take several minutes, watch carefully to prevent burning.
  5. (Optional) Add butter to the pan and toast the bottom side of the bread until crispy.
  6. Remove the toast from the pan and set aside.

Variations:

Ham and Cheese:

  1. After toasting the cheese-coated side, flip the bread over.
  2. Top with a slice of ham and a slice of cheese.
  3. Cover with another slice of bread.
  4. (Optional) Add butter to the pan and toast the bottom side of the bread until crispy.
  5. Cut in half and serve.

Egg-in-Toast

  1. Use a cup to press down on the bread slice until creating a shallow indent.
  2. Crack an egg in to the indent.
  3. Place the toast into the pan until egg is coocked.
  4. Top with ham and cheese.
  5. Cover with another slice of cheese-coated bread.
  6. (Optional) Add butter to the pan and toast the bottom side of the bread until crispy.
  7. Cut in half and serve.

Nutella:

  1. Spread Nutella on the bread.
  2. Cover with another slice of cheese-coated bread.
  3. (Optional) Add butter to the pan and toast the bottom side of the bread until crispy.
  4. Cut in half and serve.

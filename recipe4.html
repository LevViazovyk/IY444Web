Egg Spinach Tortilla "Pizza"

This recipe demonstrates a quick and easy way to make an Egg Spinach Tortilla.

Ingredients:

  • Tortilla: 25cm diameter
  • Eggs: 4-5 large
  • Tomato: 1 medium, diced
  • Onion: 1/2 medium, diced
  • Green Onion: 2-3 stalks, chopped
  • Spinach: A handful, roughly chopped
  • Bacon: 3-4 strips, cooked and crumbled
  • Salt: To taste
  • Pepper: To taste
  • Crushed Red Pepper: (Optional) A pinch for a little heat
  • Mozzarella Cheese: 200g, shredded
  • Parsley: For garnish, finely chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 200-210Â°C (adjust based on your oven).
  2. Cut parchment paper to fit inside your baking bowl.
  3. Place a tortilla in the baking bowl.Make it flat.
  4. Crack eggs into a bowl then Pour eggs onto the tortilla.
  5. Sprinkle a little salt, pepper and Crushed red pepper (if using).
  6. Chop onion, tomatos, green onion, spinach and bacon.
  7. Add onion, tomato, green onion,spinach and bacon.
  8. Cover with aluminum foil (optional)
  9. Bake for 15 minutes (adjust based on your oven).
  10. Sprinkle with Mozzarella Cheese.
  11. Bake again for 10-12 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  12. Garnish with chopped Parsley.
  13. Cut into wedges like a pizza and serve immediately.

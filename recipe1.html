Chocolate Pudding Cake

This is a Chocolate Pudding Cake recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 200g Dark Chocolate (For 1 cake)
  • 750ml Milk
  • 80g Sugar (about 6 TBSP)
  • 7g Agar Agar Powder (about 1 TBSP)
  • Milk Carton (or cake mold)

Instructions:

  1. Chop dark chocolate.
  2. Pour 500ml of Milk first.
  3. Then pour in 250ml more to the pan.
  4. Add Sugar
  5. Add Agar agar powder.
  6. Dissolve and Mix well.
  7. Start from medium-low heat, then change it to low heat in the middle.
  8. If you see steam and foam, turn off the heat.
  9. Put chopped chocolate in. Be careful with hot milk.
  10. Melt the chocolate over low heat and keep stirring.
  11. Wash and use a milk carton if you do not have a cake mold.
  12. Just cut the head of the Milk Carton.
  13. Pour into the milk carton.
  14. Stir with chopstick to remove bubbles.
  15. Cool in the refrigerator for 1-1.5 hours.
